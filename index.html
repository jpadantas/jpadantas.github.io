Joao P. A. Dantas

Research Engineer — Brazilian Air Force

I am a Research Engineer in the Decision Support Systems Subdivision of the C4ISR Division at the Institute for Advanced Studies (IEAv), part of the Brazilian Air Force. My work focuses on the development of defense technologies and aerospace applications, with research interests at the intersection of autonomy, machine learning, robotics, and simulation.

I hold a Ph.D. and M.Sc. in Electronic and Computer Engineering from the Aeronautics Institute of Technology (ITA), where I also earned my B.Sc. in Mechanical-Aeronautical Engineering, including a one-year exchange at Stony Brook University. From 2021 to 2023, I was a visiting researcher at the AirLab, part of the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University.
